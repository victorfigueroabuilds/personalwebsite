Navigating Your Network is a blog post I wrote for the Wily Network a Boston based program that seeks to empower students with the necessary tools to succeed in college and beyond. I talk about my own struggles learning to network as a student without strong relationship to the adults in my life. My goal is to help other students who have had non traditional backgrounds and have faced their own unique challenges to navigate their life.
